| Requirement | Application to Nieuwbouw | Woningpaspoort Feature |
|-------------|-------------------------|------------------------|
| **Consumentendossier** | Mandatory for all new homes | Auto-organized dossier |
| **Kwaliteitsborger** | Independent inspector required | Inspection document storage |
| **Borgingsplan** | Quality assurance plan | Plan template and tracking |
| **Risicobeoordeling** | Risk assessment per phase | Phase-based documentation |
| **20-Year Retention** | All documents kept 20 years | Cloud archive with backup |
